## Design Context

### Users
Men in Riyadh looking for Quran memorization (تحفيظ) and recitation circles. They open the app on the go — often after prayer or while planning their week — and want to find a nearby men's circle quickly, see time and place, and join without creating an account. The product is men-only.

### Brand Personality
Calm, trustworthy, welcoming. The interface should feel like a quiet mosque courtyard: clear, respectful, and easy — never flashy or social-feed-like.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Light mode first: warm sand/cream surfaces with deep Quranic green accents
- Arabic-only, RTL-first typography (IBM Plex Sans Arabic or Tajawal)
- Refined minimal: one job per screen, large tap targets, time and place first
- Soft atmosphere via subtle gradients/patterns — not flat white, not dark neon
- Anti-references: purple AI gradients, dashboard clutter, card grids of stats, English-first layouts, signup walls

### Design Principles
1. **Zero friction** — No accounts; join in one or two taps
2. **Place and time first** — Every circle row leads with when and where
3. **Calm hierarchy** — Brand name is the hero signal; one headline and one CTA per screen
4. **Arabic native** — RTL layout, Arabic copy, and type chosen for Arabic readability
5. **Restraint** — No cards in the hero, no badge clutter; motion only for entrance and join confirmation
